About Me

Fullstack Developer

At the age of 19, I am embarking on my journey into the realm of development, fueled by a longstanding fascination with understanding the intricacies of various systems—from cars and machines to computers and games. This curiosity came mainly from games like Undertale, The Binding of Isaac and content creators like Michael Reeves, Dani, Mark Rober, Victor Schiavon, Little Carlos which has led me to delve deeper into programming, where I specialize in C and GML. Additionally, I am adept at working with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.

Fluent in English, I attribute my language proficiency to a childhood immersed in video games from the age of 2, learning the language almost effortlessly. Beyond English, I invest my free time in studying Japanese and French.

My ultimate aspiration is to contribute to the world of game development, a passion that has been ingrained in me since childhood. Currently, I am engrossed in a project titled "Hellish Nightmare." This undertaking is a roguelike game akin to Hades, infused with a touch of humor, irony, and inspiration drawn from "Dante's Inferno, The Divine Comedy." The game features a floor-like inspired stage system, with each level serving a distinct purpose and contributing significantly to the overarching narrative.

As the co-founder of Imaginaction, a company dedicated to fostering innovation in diverse realms, including software and gaming, I collaborate closely with my partner, Narcizo Marques Our professional journey began during our college years, and since then, we have successfully worked together on several projects. Notable among these ventures are the development of hand-tracking software utilizing webcam technology and the creation of the Imaginaction website.
